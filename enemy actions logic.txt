IMPLEMENT enemy intents and actions so that enemies have a Turn. In the more general picture, a Slay the spire turn (which is what's displayed to the player) is comprised of:

1) Player Reset: energy = max_energy, block = 0 (or not, depending on the case).*
2) Player drawing 5 cards.*
3) Start of turn effects.
4) Player turn priority. Freedom to perform player actions such as playing cards, using potions or ending the turn.*
5) End of turn effects, either from relics, buffs, debuffs. Can apply to both enemies and players.
6) Enemy turn priority. Enemies act from top to bottom, performing each of their intents.
7) End of enemy turn. Not used for much at the moment. But could allow for debuffs to take effect at the end of enemy turn.

This loops until the fight is over!
